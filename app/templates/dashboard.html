About eTBF

The Enhanced Total Biological Fingerprint (eTBF) AI Diagnostics System integrates five biological data modalities for comprehensive diagnosis:

  • Radiology — MRI, CT, and X-ray imaging
  • Ultrasound — Real-time echogenicity and Doppler flow
  • Electrophoresis — Protein, DNA, and lipoprotein gel analysis
  • Microscopy — Whole Slide Images (histopathology)
  • Laboratory — CBC, BMP, CMP, and ABG with temporal analysis
