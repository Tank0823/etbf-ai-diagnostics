## Key Findings

### Ultrasound
- Solid, hypoechoic nodule
- Taller-than-wide shape
- Ill-defined margins
- Macrocalcifications
- TI-RADS: 5 (high suspicion)

### Histopathology
- Tumor size: 6mm
- Classical papillary thyroid microcarcinoma
- Nuclear features: Grooves, intranuclear pseudoinclusions
- Psammoma bodies
- No lymphovascular invasion
- BRAF V600E mutation positive

### Laboratory
- TSH: Normal
- Free T4: Normal
- Calcitonin: Normal

## Expected Diagnosis
Papillary Thyroid Microcarcinoma (Classical Type)
