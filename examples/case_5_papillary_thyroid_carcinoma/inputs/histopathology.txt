HISTOPATHOLOGY REPORT

Patient: 30-year-old female
Specimen: Thyroidectomy, right lobe

MACROSCOPIC:
- Specimen: Right thyroid lobe, 3.5 x 2.0 x 1.5 cm
- Lesion: Well-circumscribed nodule, 6mm in diameter
- Cut Surface: Firm, tan-white, slightly gritty

MICROSCOPIC:
- Well-circumscribed, unencapsulated nodule
- Variably sized follicles
- Papillary structures with fibrovascular cores
- Nuclear features:

  - Intranuclear pseudoinclusions
  - Ground glass chromatin
- Psammoma bodies present
- No lymphovascular invasion
- No extrathyroidal extension

IMMUNOHISTOCHEMISTRY:
- CK7: Positive
- TTF-1: Positive
- Thyroglobulin: Positive
- Calcitonin: Negative
- BRAF V600E: Positive (mutation detected)

DIAGNOSIS:
Classical Papillary Thyroid Microcarcinoma (6mm)
Stage: pT1a N0 M0
AJCC Stage I

COMMENT:
Complete excision with negative margins. Excellent prognosis.
