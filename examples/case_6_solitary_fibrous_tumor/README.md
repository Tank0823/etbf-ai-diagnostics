## Key Findings

### MRI
- Location: Left nasolabial region
- Size: 3.2 x 2.5 x 2.0 cm
- Signal: T1 isointense to muscle, T2 heterogeneously hyperintense
- Enhancement: Heterogeneous, moderate to avid
- Borders: Well-circumscribed, lobulated
- Adjacent structures: Abuts anterior nasal spine without bony erosion

### Histopathology
- Vaguely encapsulated fibrous tumor
- Spindle cells arranged haphazardly (patternless pattern)
- Collagen bundles interspersed
- Focal myxoid change
- No necrosis or significant atypia

### Immunohistochemistry
- STAT6: Strong nuclear positivity (diagnostic)
- CD34: Diffuse strong positivity
- BCL2: Positive
- Desmin: Negative
- S100: Negative
- EMA: Negative

### Molecular
- NAB2-STAT6 fusion confirmed by FISH

Solitary Fibrous Tumor
